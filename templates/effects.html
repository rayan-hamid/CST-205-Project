Effects of Climate Change

Explore the various impacts of climate change on our planet.

Rising Sea Levels

Melting glaciers and expanding seawater are causing sea levels to rise, threatening coastal communities.

Extreme Weather

Climate change increases the frequency and intensity of extreme weather events like hurricanes, floods, and droughts.

Ecosystem Disruption

Changing temperatures and weather patterns disrupt ecosystems, leading to loss of biodiversity.

Increased Wildfires

Prolonged droughts and rising temperatures have led to more frequent and severe wildfires.

Coral Bleaching

Rising ocean temperatures cause corals to lose their vibrant colors and their ability to survive.

Ocean Acidification

Increased CO2 absorption by the oceans is altering their chemistry, affecting marine life and ecosystems.

Food Security Risks

Changing weather patterns and extreme events are threatening global food production and availability.

Health Impacts

Heatwaves, air pollution, and the spread of diseases are causing serious health problems globally.

Water Scarcity

Prolonged droughts and changing precipitation patterns are leading to a growing water crisis worldwide.
